Zoning Board of Appeals

About

The Zoning Board of Appeals (ZBA) hears and decides appeals from any order, requirements, decision, or determination made by the Planning Commission or other administrative body of the City of Williamston charged with enforcement of the Zoning Ordinance.

Five Board members are appointed for 3-year terms by the Mayor with the approval of the City Council.
