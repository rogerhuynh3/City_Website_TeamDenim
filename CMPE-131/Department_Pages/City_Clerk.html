City Clerk

Duties:

  • Clerk of the Council and of each appointive board of the city.
  • Attends all meetings of the Council and of each board of the city and keep a permanent journal of its proceedings.
  • Custodian of all papers, documents, and records pertaining to the city.
  • Administer all oaths required by law and the ordinances of the city.
  • Responsible for maintaining all voter registration records and administering all elections in the city.
