Planning and Zoning

Duties

Planning and Zoning for the City is a combination of City staff and Planning Consultants. Please contact the City of Williamston to inquire if you need to

  • Fill out an application
  • Receive a permit
  • Receive approval from a City Board or Commission.
  • Staff is available Monday through Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., excluding City Holidays. We can also schedule a pre-application meeting. Please call us at 517-655-2774.
