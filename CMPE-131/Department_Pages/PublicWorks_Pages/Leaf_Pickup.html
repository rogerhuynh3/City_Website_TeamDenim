Leaf pickup

When

  • Leaf pickup is in the Fall only (no leaf pickup in the Spring). Any changes to the dates on the calendar will be on the City website and on the Cityâ€™s Facebook page. There is no specific schedule for leaves as it depends on the fall foliage and weather.

Rules

  • Do not bag your leaves.
  • Leaves must be in the street at the curb.
  • If you live on Grand River Avenue, do not put them in the street, only rake to the curb.
  • All other areas, if the leaves are not in the street, they will not be picked up.
