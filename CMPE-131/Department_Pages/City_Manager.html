City Manager

Duties

  • The City Manager is appointed by the Council.
  • City Manager is the administrative agent of the Council.
  • Is responsible to the Council for the conduct of the administrative functions and business of the city
  • Supervises and coordinates the work of the administrative officers and departments of the city; and
  • Prepares and submits to the Council the annual budget proposal of the city, together with supporting information.
