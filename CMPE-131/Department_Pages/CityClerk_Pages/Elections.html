2024 election dates

  • Tuesday, February 27, 2024 - Presidential Primary
  • Tuesday, August 6, 2024 - State Primary
  • Tuesday, November 4, 2024 - State General Election

​​​Voting options

City of Williamston registered voters, you have many opportunities to cast your vote during the February 27, 2024 Presidential Primary Election:

  • IN PERSON ON ELECTION DAY (Tue, 2.27.24) at the polling location located at the Williamston Middle School Gym (drive behind the school), 3845 Vanneter Road, Williamston.
  • VIA ABSENTEE VOTER BALLOT - You can complete an application at the Clerk’s office at City Hall, or you can submit online at https://mvic.sos.state.mi.us/AVApplication/Index
  • EARLY VOTING – all qualified voters have the right to mark a ballot and deposit the ballot into the tabulator. Early voting will be at Williamston City Hall on the dates and times listed on the attached. Questions, please contact the Clerk's office at 517-655-2774, cityclerk@williamston-mi.us. Thank you!
