Real ID Requirement

Beginning May 3, 2023, Michigan residents will need to present a REAL ID-compliant document to fly within the United States and enter certain federal facilities to comply with federal law.

Learn more about REAL ID and schedule your appointment with the Secretary of State's office at the state website.
