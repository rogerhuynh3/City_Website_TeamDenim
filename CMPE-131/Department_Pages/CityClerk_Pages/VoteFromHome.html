Want to vote from home?

  • Request an absentee ballot online at: https://mvic.sos.state.mi.us/AVApplication
  • Print and complete this application – Request an absentee ballot online at: https://williamston-mi.us/documents/absent-voter-ballot-application/ and mail or drop off to
    Williamston City Clerk,
    161 E. Grand River Ave.,
    Williamston, MI 48895
