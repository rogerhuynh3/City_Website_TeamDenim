Written Comments, Input, or Questions

PRE Form

City residents are encouraged to participate in City meetings. Comments via email are accepted until 4 p.m. on the day of the meeting and will be shared with the members of the public body as part of their agenda materials. You may submit comments/questions through the City Clerk at: cityclerk@williamston-mi.us.

​​​​Persons with Disabilities

Members of the public requiring special accommodations may contact the City Clerk’s office at 517.655.2774 ext. 102, or via e-mail to: cityclerk@williamston-mi.us. Requests made with less than two business days’ notice may not be able to be accommodated.
