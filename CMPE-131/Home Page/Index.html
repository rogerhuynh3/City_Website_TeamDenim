Planning Commission Meeting

The Planning Commission Meeting in Williamston is a vital forum where city officials, residents, and stakeholders gather to discuss and make decisions on urban development and zoning regulations. These meetings play a crucial role in shaping the city's growth and ensuring that proposed projects align with its long-term vision while preserving its unique character and quality of life. Through transparent discourse and community engagement, the Planning Commission aims to promote responsible development and encourage civic participation in the city's decision-making process.
