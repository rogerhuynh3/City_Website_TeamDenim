When is leaf pick up?

Leaf pickup occurs only in the Fall; there's no pickup in the Spring. Please refrain from bagging your leaves. Any updates to the pickup dates will be posted on the City website and Facebook page.

For residents on Grand River Avenue, avoid placing leaves in the street; instead, rake them to the curb. In all other areas, leaves must be placed in the street for pickup; if they're not in the street, they won't be collected.
