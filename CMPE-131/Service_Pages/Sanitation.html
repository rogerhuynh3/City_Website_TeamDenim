Who do I contact when my garbage has not been picked up?

For inquiries about service, reach out to Granger at 517-372-2800. If you reside south of Grand River Ave., your pick-up day is Monday; for those north of Grand River Ave., it's Wednesday.

How do I find my trash pick up day?

Have your trash and recycling out by the curb by 7:00 A.M. on the scheduled pickup day.

If there's a holiday before or on your pickup day, collection will be on the following day. Questions about service? Contact Granger at 517-372-2800.
