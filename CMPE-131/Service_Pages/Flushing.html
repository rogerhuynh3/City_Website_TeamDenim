When does the city do hydrant flushing?

Hydrant flushing is scheduled to take place throughout the City of Williamston during the spring and summer months. It occurs during the day to facilitate proper inspection and maintenance of the water system. Residents are advised to confirm that their water is running clear before doing laundry, particularly with light-colored or white fabrics.

Hydrant flushing serves the vital purpose of ensuring the functionality of the water system, ensuring it is capable of effectively combating fires when necessary.
