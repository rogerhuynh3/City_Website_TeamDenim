Who is responsible for shoveling snow and ice off the sidewalks?

Property owners are responsible for clearing any ice and snow from the entire length of the sidewalk on their property, regardless of whether the property is unoccupied or has a building or structure on it.

By when do I need to clear my sidewalk of snow and ice?

Sidewalks must be cleared within 24 hours after snowfall stops if the accumulation is less than 4 inches. If the snow accumulation exceeds 4 inches, property owners have 48 hours to clear the sidewalks.
