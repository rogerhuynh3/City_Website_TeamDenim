How do I file a police report?

To file a police report, get in touch with the Williamston Police Department at 517-655-4222 during regular business hours (Mon-Fri, 8am-5pm).

If you encounter our voicemail, leave a message at extension 201, and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.

How do I get a copy of my traffic crash report?

To obtain a copy of your crash report from incidents within the City, visit the LexisNexis website: https://policereports.lexisnexis.com/. If you require customer assistance with LexisNexis, you can reach them at 1-866-215-2771.

Who do I call about police-related non-emergencies?

To discuss a non-emergency matter with an officer or to file a report, please reach out to the Williamston Police Department at 517-655-4222 during regular business hours (Mon-Fri, 8am-5pm)

If you happen to reach our voicemail, kindly leave a message at extension 201, and we'll ensure to return your call promptly.
