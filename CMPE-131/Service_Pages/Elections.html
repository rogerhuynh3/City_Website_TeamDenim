When is the early voting?

During the early voting period, which begins the second Saturday before an election and concludes the Sunday before the election, all qualified electors have the right to mark a ballot and submit it into the tabulator.

Early voting hours are from 8:30 am to 4:30 pm and the location for early voting is:

Williamston City Hall

161 E Grand River Ave

Williamston, MI 48895
