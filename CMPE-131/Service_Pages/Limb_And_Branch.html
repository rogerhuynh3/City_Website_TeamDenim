When is limb/branch cleanup?

Collection dates are scheduled for the 1st and 3rd Tuesdays from April through November, courtesy of the City’s Department of Public Works, and there's no extra cost to residents.

  • This service is for fallen branches or minor tree trimming conducted by the property owner. The City won't collect brush or branches from trees removed by a professional service.
  • Place brush piles neatly at the curb, ensuring they're no higher than 4 feet and all cut ends face the same direction, preferably away from the traffic flow.
  • This service is for large branches and limbs exceeding 4 feet in length, with the main branch stem having a minimum diameter of 1 inch and not exceeding 6 inches. (To safeguard employees, very small branches or sticks won't be collected.)
  • Brush contaminated with dirt, vines, stumps, stones, or metal won't be collected as it can't be chipped.
